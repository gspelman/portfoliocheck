About

My background is in Communication, Graphic Design, and Technical Recruitment. Currently, I'm a Graduate student at the University of Michigan School of Information with a specialization in UX design. Through my experience I've developed a strong background in research, conducting interviews, communication, and understanding design principles.

I was driven into design because I felt it balanced my innate creative sense, analytical desire of organization, and love of research. Additionally, design boasts the reward of solving problems while making a difference in people's lives.
